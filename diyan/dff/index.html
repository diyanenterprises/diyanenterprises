About Diyan Enterprises

Diyan Enterprises is a trusted manufacturer specializing in high-quality paver blocks and curb stones. Located strategically near Shiv Kunj Ashram on Talaja Main Road, Adhewada, Bhavnagar, we have established ourselves as a reliable partner in the construction and infrastructure industry.

Our commitment to excellence drives us to manufacture superior rubber molded precast paving blocks and curb stones that meet the highest standards of quality and durability. We take pride in our comprehensive approach, offering not just manufacturing but complete project solutions.

Our Products

Rubber Molded Precast

High-quality paving blocks manufactured using advanced rubber molding technology. Available in various designs, sizes, and specifications to suit different project requirements. Durable, weather-resistant, and aesthetically pleasing.

Central & Garden Curb Stones

Precision-manufactured curb stones for infrastructure and landscaping projects. Custom sizes available for roads, gardens, borders, and pathways with superior durability.

Zigzag Paver Blocks

Interlocking zigzag design paver blocks for driveways, walkways, and decorative surfaces. Superior grip, water drainage, and aesthetic appeal with exceptional durability.
